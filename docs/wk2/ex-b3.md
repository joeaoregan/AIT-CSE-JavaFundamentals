## Java Fundamentals exercises
### Wk2 – Ex B3

Write a program called MathsOps. Take in two integer values from the user; store in num1 and num2. Using “good” variable names/indentation,  calculate the sum, product, difference and quotient of the two numbers. Output your results. In the format: The sum of 3 and 5 is 8 (assuming 3 and 5 are typed in). Ensure that you do not divide by 0 (as this will cause an exception i.e. a runtime error). 

> Enter num1: 3  
> Enter num2: 5  
> The sum of 3 and 5 is 8  
> The product of 3 and 5 is 15  
> The difference of 3 and 5 is -2  
> The quotient of 3 and 5 is 0

> Enter num1: 5  
> Enter num2: 0  
> The sum of 5 and 0 is 5  
> The product of 5 and 0 is 0  
> The difference of 5 and 0 is 5  
> num2 is 0; cannot divide by 0 : 0
